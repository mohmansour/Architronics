About Us

“Smart Home” is an automation system that allows the home inhabitant to remotely control the appliances from any room or any location worldwide by phone or internet, and provides a security system that protect home owners from any unexpected incidents i.e. Fire, Robbery…etc. Make it very easy for the owners to monitor and watch their home in order to make them feel safe.
